Project Desscription: Campus Bathroom Rating [Mobile or Web] App

An application to keep track of info and ratings for bathrooms - specifically
on campus, but could be expanded to include other places as well in the future.
Desired features are:
	- Location
	- Whether the bathroom is gender neutral, Men's, or Women's
	- Handicap Accessibility
	- Cleanliness rating
	- Maintenence rating
	- Overall bathroom rating
Extra features:
	- When does the bathroom open/close?
	- Is there a changing table?
	- Dispensers
	- Showers
	- Sinks in stall(s)
	- Mirrors
	- Urinal partitians
	- Changing Rooms
	- Lockers
	- Comment section
	- Next nearest bathroom
	- Paper towels and/or dryers
